HGTFS feed — US railroad network, 1840-1870. Conforms to https://hgtfs.github.io/
Source: RRMMA (Railroads and the Making of Modern America), U. Nebraska-Lincoln.
Temporal model is per-entity (date_opened + date_precision uncertainty brackets);
network growth lives in network_edges.txt (each edge dated). route_type=1405
(Steam railway). trips/stop_times are RECONSTRUCTED structure (time_accuracy=1),
not archival timetables. See ../HGTFS.md. Regenerate: python3 build_hgtfs.py
